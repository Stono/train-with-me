Train With Me is designed to be a social application, therefore we need you to login with a social provider.
In doing so, we will request some permissions, and we want to explain why we and how we use that data.
  • Email: We need your email to create a unique account for you.
  • Profile: We use this to get your name and your picture.
  • Friends: We use this to help you find other friends using TWM.
We take your privacy very seriously, so would like to assure you that your data is only used to enhance your experience in this app, and never passed on to anyone else without your explicit permission.
